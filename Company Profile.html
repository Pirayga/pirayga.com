Company Information

Pirayga Import & export Company (Pvt) Ltd is a family owned company based a few kilometers from the scenic city of Narammala in Sri Lanka. We have been selling construction equipment for over 20 years and have continually added to our portfolio of products and services in order meet the growing demands of Sri Lanka's fast growing construction projects over the past few years. Pirayga provides the complete package of equipment, services and spares at affordable prices. Our main aim is to provide value for money and you can rely on us to give you an honest service. We are constantly striving to improve our services and pride ourselves for providing an excellent customer service with custom after sales services tailored to individual needs.
